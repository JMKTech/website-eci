About ECI Services

ECI Services is a general construction and specialty contractor providing services throughout the Western United States. We provide prime construction services on Federal projects, as well as specialty construction services to Federal, municipal and industrial clients and general contractors.

If you have prime or specialty subcontracting work, we welcome the opportunity to work with your company.

Our Location

ECI Services' corporate headquarters are located in Pasco, Washington at the Port of Pasco Industrial Center. Rail, barge or highway transportation all provide access to our facility. The Port provides the capability to offload structures up to 45 tons.

Our capabilities include:
  • General Construction Services– Civil – Mechanical – Remodel – Renovation – Design/Build Environmental Services
  • Industrial Painting and Specialty Coatings
  • Industrial Vacuum and Hydro Excavation
